Our story

Babett and Karolina, both mothers of Kindergarten and school children, have noticed that there is no any financial literacy training, workshops etc for teenengers and teachers at german schools.

There is a lack of financial education and knowledge at schools. They decided to work on it and together with Dragos (IT) create an online learning tool for teachers, teenengers in german schools.

The first online financial literacy learning platform for teenagers, teachers and schools.

For Schools

Enrich your curriculum by offering finacial literacy workshops and courses leveraging on state of art technology.

For Teachers

Schulgold provides you with engaging content and material tu support your financial literay program in class.

For Teenagers

With SchulGold, teenagers learn money management skills essential to an independent, happier and fulfilling life.

Karolina Decker

Karolina is a co-founder & CEO of FinMarie - the first online investment platform for women in Europe. She has over ten years experience in the financial sector, initially as a manager at DnB NORD (real estate financing), with responsibility over 20 employees and financing volume of 250 M.€ per year. Later during her time with Deutsche Bank in Berlin (Compliance), she noticed that female clients ask different questions than men and have a different approach to wealth creation.

As soon as she recognized the problem, she started working on a solution. The mother of two children started the regular series of events with a colleague in August 2017 Mind the Gap e.V for women to learn about their finances. The Meetup and seminar format on the subject „Women & Wealth” is now held nationwide. The “Mind the Gap” community comprises more than 3000 women and is growing continuously.



Babett Mahnert

Babett is a founder, mom and financial coach. She has advised over 10,000 clients in 4 different banks over 17 years. Her customers included board members, entrepreneurs, politicians and musicians.

Babett Mahnert founded her company Goldfrau in February 2019 to support women with joy and sustainability to take care of their finances. Her vision is for women to build a financially free life so that they can pass on this valuable experience to their children. "Lets talk about money, honey".
